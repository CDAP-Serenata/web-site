Literature Survey




Welcome to HarmonAIze

At HarmonAIze, we're on a mission to democratize vocal training through AI. Founded by music enthusiasts and tech experts, our skilled team harnesses cutting-edge machine learning to create a personalized, self-learning platform. We offer tailored exercises, feedback, and training plans catering to diverse voice types and skill levels. Our innovative talent discovery features connect aspiring singers globally with music professionals seeking new talent. Driven by passion for music and technological excellence, we foster an inclusive community where creativity thrives and dreams are realized through the power of AI.

At the core of HarmonAIze lies an advanced suite of AI algorithms that evaluate pitch accuracy, rhythm, vocal technique, and expression, offering real-time feedback and targeted recommendations. Our platform adapts to each user's voice type, skill level, and preferred genre, curating personalized exercise plans and vocal warm-ups.

HarmonAIze introduces groundbreaking features like lip-syncing detection, vocal register enhancement, and pronunciation guidance for various languages. Additionally, our recruitment feature allows singers to create profiles showcasing their achievements, enabling music professionals to discover and hire promising vocal talents from around the globe. With HarmonAIze, singers can learn, grow, and thrive in the music industry through an AI-driven approach that streamlines the learning process and makes singing evaluation cost-effective and efficient. Unlock your true singing potential with the power of artificial intelligence.

Research Gap




1
Automatic singing evaluation is already available even as Karoake games. But we offer something different

Aims to enhance musical talent through AI technology, focusing on the integration of advanced machine learning techniques into music education to provide personalized feedback and analysis tools. The research component explores several key areas. Audio Analysis Techniques: utilizing signal processing algorithms to extract features such as pitch, rhythm, and timbre from audio recordings for comparison against reference performances. Machine Learning Models: employing deep learning models to recognize complex musical patterns and gestures, offering targeted feedback on specific performance aspects. Educational Theories and Self-Regulated Learning (SRL): integrating principles of SRL and examining various feedback modalities (visual, auditory, multimodal) to support self-guided learning and enhance learning outcomes. User-Centric Design: investigating the design and implementation of user-friendly interfaces and interaction models, considering user experience, engagement, and motivational factors to create an effective and engaging learning environment. The project's objective is to develop a sophisticated, personalized learning tool that significantly improves the practice and performance of singers.

2
Voice Type Identification and Exercise Personalization

Accurately identifying each user's voice type is crucial for personalized learning. Existing systems struggle with handling diverse voice types and accents. HarmonAIze aims to develop a robust voice type identification system using advanced machine learning techniques and large datasets.

Providing personalized exercises tailored to the user's voice type, skill level, and goals is essential. Current singing tutor apps often offer generic exercises that may not effectively target individual strengths and weaknesses. HarmonAIze will incorporate advanced skill level assessment algorithms to accurately evaluate proficiency across various singing techniques. By combining voice type identification and skill level assessment, our platform will offer personalized exercise plans that continuously adapt to the user's progress, ensuring an engaging and effective learning experience.

3
Authenticity detection

Identifying whether the exercise is performed by the exact person who claims to be is necessary for singing evaluations. It's more like preventing cheating on an exam.

4
Identifying the impact of Singing’s disease on the speaking activities of individuals by tracking their vocal frequencies

The normal speaking style of the individuals would get directly impacted from the development of Singing symptoms. When it comes to the functionalities offered by this specific system, the identification of the Singing through the vocal frequency data and forwarding those patients on some preventive measures could be considered as very important ones. Here at first, the vocal frequency data of the Singing patient will be captured while he or she is speaking in a noraml setting. These data will be then converted and fed to the system, thus declaring any symptomatic signs of the Singing on the patient. Also, some treatment recommendations would come all along the way from the system, in case if any trace of the disease is found in the vocal frequency data.

Research Problem & Solution




Proposed Problem

Aspiring singers face numerous challenges in their pursuit of vocal development and music careers. Access to quality training is limited, with skilled coaches concentrated in urban areas and high costs creating financial barriers. Existing online resources are predominantly English-based, hindering global reach due to language barriers. Traditional group lessons lack personalized feedback tailored to individual voice types and skill levels, risking improper training.

Talent discovery relies heavily on personal connections and localized auditions, overlooking diverse talents from various backgrounds. Subjective evaluations by human judges introduce biases and inconsistencies, hindering fair assessments of abilities. HarmonAIze aims to address these obstacles through AI and machine learning technologies, providing personalized vocal training, objective performance evaluation, and efficient talent discovery. Our platform empowers singers worldwide to overcome hurdles and unlock their full potential in the music industry.

Proposed Solution

HarmonAIze revolutionizes vocal training through an AI-driven, personalized self-learning platform tailored to each singer's needs. Advanced AI algorithms evaluate pitch, rhythm, technique, and expression, providing real-time feedback and targeted recommendations.

Voice analysis enables personalized exercise plans and warm-ups based on the user's voice type, skill level, and preferred genre, allowing focused progression. Groundbreaking features include lip-syncing detection for authentic performance evaluation, vocal register enhancement for seamless register integration, and pronunciation guidance across languages. The innovative recruitment feature enables singers to create comprehensive talent profiles, empowering music professionals to discover and hire promising vocalists globally. By combining AI personalization, objective evaluation, language inclusivity, and efficient talent discovery, HarmonAIze streamlines the learning process cost-effectively. The engaging platform fosters a supportive community, motivating consistent skill development.
